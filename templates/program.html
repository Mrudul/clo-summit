What can you expect?

CLO Summit India.
Date: 20th November 2009
Location: Mumbai
Venue: TISS Convention Center, TATA Institute Of Social Sciences, Deonar.
Timing: 9:00 am to 7:00 pm (Followed by networking dinner)

A day filled with inspiring keynotes, a few crisp panel discussions, stimulating presentations. And a few pleasant surprises.

In a fast changing economic environment with increasing competitive intensity, how can organizational learning become a source of competitive advantage and continuous change?

How can organizational learning, positively impact the business and proactively propel change in business strategy or ensure its effective implementation, instead of just reacting to it?

How can organizations use learning as a tool to innovate, differentiate themselves in order to attract and retain Talent, Customers and Investors?

The inaugural CLO summit shall endeavour to find answers to these questions and the following key areas through interaction and debate amongst some of the best minds in the field.

  • Agenda ⇓
  • Speakers ⇓
    • Organizational Learning: A source of competitive advantage and continuous change. Strategic perspective from the Leadership (CEOs, Presidents)
    • 'Getting a seat at the table': The role of the CLO in organizations; The role of L&D. The current state of learning & development function within organizations: A look at best practices and needs for the future.
    • Learning: public sector and non corporate organizations. PSUs, defence services, NGOs/voluntary sector, government, academia: Current best practices and organizational learning needs given the socio economic environment.
    • Developing Learning Content and delivery: Demographics of learners, Technology, Channels/Medium, Reach, Cost. The internet and online learning platforms, e-learning, distance education.
    • Organizational Learning on a budget and The ROI challenge.
    • On the Job Learning, apprenticeship, Coaching and Mentoring: Industry & Organizational Successes
    • Learning and R&D: Role of Learning in R&D intensive organisations
    • Career Paths, Domain expertise and Learning. From Functional Titles to Domain Titles and experts?
    • Learning in multicultural environments: Conglomerates, Multi National Corporations

  • The final program along with the details of speakers shall be available soon as the CLO Summit Team endeavours to ensure an eclectic mix of thought leaders.
